

View permissions:
    mac: stat -f "%OLp" <file>
    linux: stat --format '%a' <file>


Changing permissions:
    chmod u/g/o/a +/- r/w/x

    u: user
    g: group
    o: other
    a: all

    +: give permissions
    -: remove permissions

    r: read    (4)
    w: write   (2)
    x: execute (1)
